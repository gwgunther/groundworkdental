Our take

Your practice has genuine competitive advantages—Dr. Chang's FACD credential and specialized cleft/craniofacial expertise are rare and valuable—but your site isn't capturing the search traffic or patient confidence these strengths deserve. Your biggest opportunity is adding visual before-and-afters, an FAQ page answering common patient questions, and educational content around your specialty services; doing this would help prospective patients and referring doctors find you online and understand why you're worth the trip to Long Beach. Your mobile speed also needs attention (56/100 is slow enough to lose patients mid-visit), but fixing content first will return more immediate patient inquiries.
